The Stay

Your calm coastal base between La Punta and Zicatela

Casa Ba'wi Apartemento 8 is a private two-bedroom apartment in Tamarindos, one of Puerto Escondido's most peaceful beachside pockets. Set just steps from Tamarindo Beach, it combines ocean views, warm Mexican architecture, private balconies, air-conditioned bedrooms, and a full kitchen for stays that feel easy, private, and refined.

Location

Quiet Tamarindos location, seconds from the beach

Casa Ba'wi is located in Tamarindos, a quiet beachside area positioned between La Punta and Zicatela. You are around 30 seconds from Tamarindo Beach, 2 minutes from Sandala for coffee and lunch, 7 minutes from Nasiva, around 15 minutes walking to La Punta, and around 20 minutes walking to the Zicatela clubs area.
